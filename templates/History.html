    • Our History

    Gen-Z Zoological Park is one of the oldest zoos in India, established in 1959. The zoo aims to provide a safe and healthy environment for wild animals, as well as to educate the public about the importance of conservation. Over the years, the zoo has become a popular attraction for tourists and locals alike, with a diverse range of animals including tigers, lions, elephants, and many more.
