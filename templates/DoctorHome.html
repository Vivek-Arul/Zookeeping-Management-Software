Welcome to Animal Doctor

We provide veterinary care and support for a variety of animals, including:

  • Elephants
  • Giraffes
  • Lions
  • Tigers
  • Zebras
  • And many more!

Our dedicated team of animal doctors work hard every day to ensure that our animal residents are healthy and happy. From routine checkups and vaccinations to emergency care and surgeries, we strive to provide the best possible care for our animals.

If you are interested in scheduling an appointment for your animal or learning more about our veterinary services and programs, please don't hesitate to contact us. We would love to hear from you!
